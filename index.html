About

In 2014, Prof Oommen was appointed senior lecturer in English at the Kerala State Civil Service Academy Trivandrum, where he spent eight years teaching communicative English to a lot of the brilliant bureaucrats of anxious to serve the nation!

Nowadays Prof Oommen spends lion’s shaped his time with SCST students preparing them for KAS Civil Service Exam and PSC examination.
